Portion Guidelines and Recommendations

Grains: Limit to ½ cup cooked or 1 small roti/idli per meal.

Proteins: 60g of lean protein (fish/chicken) or ½ cup legumes per meal.

Vegetables: Fill half your plate with non-starchy vegetables.

Fruits: Limit to 1 small fruit or ½ cup chopped fruit per serving.

Fats: Use minimal oil (1 tsp) for cooking.

Hydration: Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily. Include herbal teas and buttermilk.

Timing: Eat at regular intervals; don't skip meals. Last meal should be 2-3 hours before bedtime.
