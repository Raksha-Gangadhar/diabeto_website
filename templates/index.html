About Us

We're a team of college students committed to preventing diabetes through early detection.

Diabeto was founded in 2025 with the mission to reduce the global impact of diabetes through predictive analytics. Our platform uses machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of health records to identify individuals at risk before symptoms appear.

With early detection, lifestyle changes and medical interventions can prevent or delay the onset of type 2 diabetes in up to 58% of high-risk individuals.

Our team combines medical expertise with ML technology to provide accurate, personalized risk assessments that empower individuals to make informed health decisions. We believe in a future where diabetes is no longer a global epidemic, and we're working every day to make that vision a reality.
